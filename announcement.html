Announcement

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused to players who were unable to play the game on the latest Android devices.

The Android 64-bit version has now been uploaded to the Google Play Store, and you can once again play the game.

However, in order to ensure stable support for Android 64-bit, the Cloth Simulation feature has been unavoidably removed. For users who were previously playing the 32-bit version and wish to continue using this feature, the previous version can be downloaded from here and installed manually. Please note that due to security or compatibility issues, installation may not be possible on some devices.

Thank you.
