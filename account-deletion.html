Account and Data Deletion Request

If you would like to request deletion of your account and associated data for this game, please contact us using the email below.

Email: proj-ten@10inter.kr

Information to include

Please include the following information in your request so we can identify your account:

  • Player ID (available in Settings > Game Info in the game)
  • Login method used (Facebook Login, etc.)

What happens when you request deletion

  • Your game account will be permanently deleted.
  • Any associated game progress and stored data will be removed.
  • This action cannot be undone.

Processing time

We will process account deletion requests within 7 days after verifying the request.

Note: Uninstalling the app does not automatically delete your account or associated data.
